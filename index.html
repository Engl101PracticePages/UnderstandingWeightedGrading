How and Why Your Canvas Grade Changes

In this course, Canvas uses weighted assignment groups. That means not every category affects your total grade equally. Use the sliders below to test different score combinations and see how your overall grade changes.

What matters most here?

The largest category in this course is Final Essays, which counts for 30% of the total grade. On Canvas, those finals are listed together as one assignment group, but in this class that group includes two 100-point final essays.

Because both final essays are worth the same number of points inside that group, each final essay effectively counts as 15% of the total course grade.
